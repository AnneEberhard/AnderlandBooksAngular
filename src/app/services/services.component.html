Formatting

You've written a wonderful story, but formatting is just not your thing? Yet, you want your manuscript to be perfect, or you even want to self-publish it - e-Book, paperback or hardcover? Proofreading of German manuscripts

At some point, you simply become blind to your work - when you have written a text yourself, you no longer notice the small mistakes. We offer a comprehensive proofreading service for German manuscripts. Prices vary according to requirements. We deliver a track-changes and a clean version in MSWord.
